About me

I'm from Fargo, ND in the U.S.A.

In 2015, I started attending North Dakota State University as a Physics major. During my undergraduate, I had opportunities to conduct research. In the summer of 2017, I had a data analyst internship at the NASA Goddard Space Flight Center where I looked at ion and electron data from the Rosetta Mission using Python. In 2019, I received my bachelor's of science in Physics.

After university, I wanted an opportunity to live abroad and experience another culture, so from 2019-2021, I particpated in the Japan Exchange and Teaching (JET) program. During this program, I lived in Kumamoto City and worked as an Assistant Language Teacher teaching English to Japanese high school students.

From 2021-2024, I moved back to the U.S.A. and worked at Amazon.

Now, I recently moved back to Japan and am living in Kumamoto City again.
